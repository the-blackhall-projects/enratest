Get a BER

A straightforward process—choose the correct service, book the visit, and receive your documents by email.

Book your BER assessment
We arrange a dwelling survey and issue the BER certificate and related reports. If the home has been extensively renovated, please have any relevant documentation (insulation, heating and ventilation details) available.
Learn more

Step-by-step

  1. Review pricing.
  2. Read our terms & conditions.
  3. Contact us with your property type, location, and preferred timeframe.
  4. We complete the dwelling survey and perform DEAP calculations.
  5. We issue your BER certificate and advisory report, and send your documents by email.
Fast turnaround
If you need a BER quickly, tell us your deadline at the time of booking.
